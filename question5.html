Is there a difference in the type of pothole service request by year and overall?


We analyzed the type of pothole request by year and overall . The three types are Residential, Major, and Alley. From the charts above it is seen that the majority of requests were for Residential. I used a Chi-square test to evaluate the null hypothesis that requests were equal across each type. The results for overall were X2 (2, N = 4,157) = 4383.31, p <.05., establishing that we can reject the null hypothesis and our results are statistically significant. The number of pothole requests is not equal across types.
